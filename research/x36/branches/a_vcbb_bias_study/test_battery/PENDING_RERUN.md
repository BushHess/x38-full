## Validation framework fixes (2026-03-16, session 6) — methodological

28. **PSR demoted from binding gate to diagnostic** — `validation/decision.py`,
    `validation/suites/selection_bias.py`, `validation/thresholds.py`,
    `docs/validation/decision_policy.md`.
    PSR (Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio) treats sr_benchmark as a known constant,
    which is anti-conservative for 2-strategy comparison (ignores baseline
    estimation error). This made it methodologically incorrect as a binding
    gate for "candidate beats baseline". Paired evidence from WFO Wilcoxon +

    pairwise superiority.

    - PBO overfitting check decoupled from PSR, remains binding soft gate (method=pbo only)
    - Method fallback check unchanged (binding soft gate)
    - PSR reported with advisory levels: strong (≥0.95), moderate (≥0.90), warning (<0.90)
    **Impact**: Strategies that previously got HOLD due to PSR < 0.95 alone will
    now be evaluated on WFO + Bootstrap evidence only. All validation results stale.
