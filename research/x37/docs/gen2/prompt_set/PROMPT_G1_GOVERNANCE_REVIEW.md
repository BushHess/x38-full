# PROMPT G1 - Governance Review Execution (Use this after G0 in the same chat)

Proceed with governance review under the active constitution.

## Mission

Determine whether the current constitution should remain active or whether a new major constitution version is justified.

## Hard instructions
- Do not perform strategy search.
- Do not introduce new candidate systems.
- Do not rerun discovery on old data.
- Evaluate only governance questions:
  - Is the objective still aligned with deployable reality?
  - Is the admitted data domain still appropriate?
  - Are the hard constraints too loose or too strict?
  - Is the complexity law causing systematic harm?
  - Is the meta-knowledge policy causing harmful lock-in?
  - Is the forward decision law causing role churn or excessive delay?
  - Is the forward evaluation evidence exposing a constitution bug?

## Required output sections
1. `Governance Findings`
2. `Constitution Still Fit?`
3. `If No, What Must Change?`
4. `Decision Recommendation`
   - `KEEP CURRENT VERSION`
   - or `PREPARE NEXT MAJOR VERSION`

## Important

Do not package yet. Packaging is the next prompt.
